Vision and Mission


Vision


Our strategy is to provide a comprehensive set of livelihood promotion services to rural households under one umbrella. Oli Vidiyal envision to serve One lakh clients by 2024, with a mission to provide comprehensive To become the most relevant, affordable and effective sustainable financial services to the economically active but underprivileged segments.


Mission


Our mission is to promote sustainable livelihoods Olividiyal aims to be part of the country's inclusive development journey through effective delivery of financial services to the excluded population. Olividiyal believes that economic development of the poor is achieved through delivery of holistic, customized and a wide array for the rural women, through financial services in a cost effective manner.
